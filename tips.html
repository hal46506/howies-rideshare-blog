Driver Tips & Gear

Simple, tested, no fluff. Some links can be affiliate later.

Load-out · Gear

Phone mount that actually stays put

Go with a telescoping arm, dash or windshield mount, and magnetic head. Look for ball-joint adjustability and a 3M pad backup for bumpy roads.

Flow · Apps

Silence notifications during rides

Use Android “Do Not Disturb” tied to your rideshare app start, so navigation and calls still come through, but pings don’t.

Peak hours · Strategy

Notre Dame game days

Pre-stage near exits, not the stadium. Set a floor price on long pickups; light traffic beats deadhead miles.
